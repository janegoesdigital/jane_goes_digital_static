Jane.Goes.Digital does Law.Goes.Digital

I am a lawyer who has learned to code. I am also of the vintage where desktop computers were a novelty and mobile phones looked like small bricks. I am also an experienced legal knowledge leader and innovator. I built this website, so it goes to show, that you can teach an old dog to learn new tricks!

This blog started as a record of my journey/struggles in ditching my job, and spending 3 months in a class room learning how to code. This, in itself, was an extension of my passion for designing and implementing legal technology. For my original blog about learning to code you can see my "beautiful" Wordpress site. I see this current blog as a place to record my thoughts about the digital transformation and it's impact on not just the way we practice law, but the nature of our laws themselves.

Enjoy!
